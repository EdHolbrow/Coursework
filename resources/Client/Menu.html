The Memory Game


This game is about trying to get the lowest score possible. You get four cards, only two of which you know.
Each card has a value, numbered cards are worth their value, e.g. 6 of hearts is 6 points. Queens and Jacks
are worth 10 points each as well as 10 numbered cards, aces are worth 1 point and Kings and pairs of numbered
cards are worth 0 points; e.g. a pair of 2s is worth 0 points. At the end of the round the player with the
lowest scoring hand wins, so the aim of the game is to memorise what cards you have in order to minimise your score.
When playing with one person the amount of turns you get depends on the difficulty selected, the harder the difficulty the fewer turns you get.
